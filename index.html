Amrita vishwa vidyapeetham

  Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amrita University) is a premier private deemed-to-be-university in India

         known for its strong emphasis on research, value-based education, and high academic rankings.

          Founded by spiritual leader Mata Amritanandamayi (Amma) in 1994, it has grown into a multi-campus

          multi-disciplinary institution with a significant national and international reputation.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a premier multi-campus, multi-disciplinary research university in India. Accredited with NAAC 'A++' grade, the university is known for academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and value-based education. Amrita offers a wide range of programs in engineering, medicine, management, arts, and sciences across its campuses.
